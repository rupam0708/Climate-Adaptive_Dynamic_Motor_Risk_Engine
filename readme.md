Climate-Adaptive Dynamic Motor Risk Engine 🌧️🚗
================================================

### **Project Overview**

This project bridges the gap between **Environmental Data Science** and **Actuarial Risk Assessment**. Unlike traditional static insurance pricing, this engine calculates **Dynamic Premiums** by correlating real-time weather risks (Rainfall, Wind Speed) with vehicle safety specifications (NCAP Ratings, ADAS features).

### **Key Features**

-   **Dynamic Risk Indexing:** A weighted algorithm that calculates a 0-1 risk probability for every policyholder.

-   **Automated Premium Lift:** Logic that adjusts base premiums (₹10,000) based on live environmental "Surcharges."

-   **Safety ROI Validation:** Proof that higher vehicle safety scores directly correlate to lower claim probabilities.

-   **Interactive Tableau Dashboard:** A 13-chart "Executive Command Center" for portfolio monitoring.

* * * * *

### **Tech Stack**

-   **Language:** Python 3.x (Google Colab)

-   **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

-   **Visualization:** Tableau Public

-   **Data Source:** Synthetic Insurance Dataset (Engineered for Indian Monsoon Scenarios)

* * * * *

### **Mathematical Framework**

The model utilizes a custom weighted Risk Score (R):

R=(w1​⋅Rainfall)+(w2​⋅Wind)-(w3​⋅Safety Score)

Where:

-   w1​,w2​: Environmental risk coefficients.

-   w3​: Vehicle safety mitigation factor.

### **Business Insights**

1.  **Underpricing Alert:** Identified regions where flat-rate pricing leads to a 15% loss ratio during monsoon peaks.

2.  **Market Segmentation:** Proved that SUV segments (C2) require higher "Climate Buffers" compared to entry-level hatchbacks.

3.  **Incentivization:** Recommends a 5-8% "Safety Discount" for vehicles with 5-star NCAP ratings to improve portfolio quality.
